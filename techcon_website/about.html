Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for local developers and has since evolved into an internationally recognized technology conference. Over the years, it has served as a platform for groundbreaking announcements, tech innovation showcases, and influential networking opportunities.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire, educate, and connect people passionate about technology. We strive to provide inclusive spaces for learning, foster innovation across industries, and support future leaders in tech through collaboration and community.

Notable Past Speakers

We’ve been honored to host some of the most influential minds in technology:

  • Alex Rivera – Renowned AI researcher, spoke on ethical AI and its role in society.
  • Lin Zhang – Cybersecurity leader and author, shared groundbreaking methods in digital defense.
  • Samira Patel – Tech entrepreneur and advocate for diversity in STEM, delivered a talk on inclusive innovation.
